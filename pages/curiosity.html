5 weird facts we bet you didn't know about human body!

The human body is an extraordinary masterpiece of nature, an intricately designed marvel that continues to captivate and astound scientists, researchers, and curious minds alike. From the microscopic intricacies of our cells to the grand symphony of our organ systems working in harmony, the human body is a testament to the wonders of biological engineering.

  1. Your eyes blink around 20 times a minute. That's over ten million times a year!
  2. Your ears never stop growing!
  3. The tongue is covered in about 8,000 taste-buds, each containing up to 100 cells helping you taste your food!
  4. The average nose produces about a cupful of nasal mucus every day!
  5. The only muscle that never tires is the heart.
